chipotle essay

I love their burrito bowl, which is basically everything you could ask for from a burrito without the tortilla. I find that they are much easier to eat, and the portions are much bigger than some other burrito bowls I've eaten (*cough  cough  schoolfood  cough  cough*).

Chipotle restaurants are always adding new items to their menu. For example, they recently added smoked brisket to their menu.

However, my favorite parts of the burrito bowl are the toppings, though i never get the new limited time toppings. I like their guacamole, which is visibly fresh and super creamy and the fajita veggies which are multicolored peppers and onions that taste really good. The thing that sets Chipotle apart from their competitors is their use of fresh ingredients that look like they were prepared on the spot. Chipotle doesn't mess around with their uses of spices either, unlike some other burrito bowls I know (*cough  cough  hack  cough  hack  hack  cough  cough  cough  schoolfood  cough  cough*)
